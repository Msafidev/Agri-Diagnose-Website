What Drives Our Research

We combine AI, plant pathology, agronomy, and climate intelligence to improve crop health for Kenyan farmers.

Plant Disease Modeling

We collect and label thousands of Kenyan crop leaf images to train accurate disease recognition models for maize, tomatoes, potatoes, beans, and horticultural crops.

Climate-Smart Analytics

Our research integrates real weather data, soil patterns, and seasonal trends to deliver adaptive farming recommendations.

Low-Data Machine Learning

We design AI models that work well with limited datasets and run smoothly on low-end Kenyan smartphones.

Current Research Projects

1. Kenyan Leaf Dataset Expansion
